Travel Tips

What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen)
  • Water bottle
  • Camera for photos
  • Light, modest clothing for temples

Important Notes

  • Shoes must be removed at religious sites
  • Photography may be restricted in some areas
  • Local guides are recommended
  • Best to start early to avoid heat
  • Carry cash for entrance fees
